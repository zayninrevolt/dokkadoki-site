Tokonatsu returns for another unforgettable celebration of Japanese pop culture from 6–10 August 2026 at Boyd Campsite, Henlow, Bedfordshire. Far from your typical indoor convention, Tokonatsu is a unique open-air festival where anime, manga, cosplay, gaming, and traditional Japanese culture come together beneath the summer sky.

## What to expect

Over the four-day weekend, fans can dive into a wide range of activities designed to suit every kind of attendee — from the relaxed to the adventurous. Enjoy anime screenings, unwind with the manga library, or join hands-on creative panels exploring crafts, art, and fandom topics.

## Cosplay & community

Cosplay is at the heart of Tokonatsu, with a cosplay masquerade, Ready Steady Cosplay challenges, and even a dedicated tent stocked with supplies for last-minute fixes. The natural outdoor setting also offers a stunning backdrop for photoshoots — perfect for capturing your best looks.

## TokoMart & artisans

Explore the TokoMart, a lively hub filled with independent artists, dealers, and a Bring & Buy area where fans can trade or sell their own creations. You'll find one-of-a-kind artwork, handmade crafts, and authentic Japanese-inspired merchandise — all while supporting small creators.

## Matsuri magic & outdoor fun

As night falls, the festival transforms into a Japanese matsuri-style celebration, complete with traditional games, food stalls, and performances. For those seeking more action, there's the legendary Otaku Royale water battle, tug-of-war contests, and plenty of open-air team games that bring everyone together in friendly competition.

## Camping & glamping

Part of the Tokonatsu charm lies in its camping community. Attendees pitch their tents side by side, sharing stories and snacks late into the night — or you can upgrade to glamping for a bit of extra comfort. Whether you're a first-timer or a long-time attendee, you'll quickly feel part of the family.
