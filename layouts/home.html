What is Dokkadoki?

Somewhere to slow down
and get lost in a story

Part café, part reading room, all comfort. Grab a drink, pull a volume off the wall, and settle into a cosy corner of The Millgate.

  • ☕ Speciality coffee, Japanese sodas, matcha and sweet snacks
  • 📚 A wall of 1,000+ manga (and growing) to read in store - browse the catalogue
  • 🛋️ Snug bench seating to curl up with a book, drink in hand
  • 🌸 A warm, kawaii-tinged space celebrating Japanese stories and everyday charm
📚
